Spinach, Tofu and Sesame Stir-Fry

By: Martha Rose Shulman

Yield: 3 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ pound tofu, cubed
  • 1 large garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 16-ounce bag fresh spinach
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Preparation

Step 1

Heat the olive oil over medium-high heat in a large skillet, then add the tofu. Stir-fry until golden brown, about 4 minutes. Add garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes, cooking for another minute. Stir in soy sauce.

Step 2

Add the spinach and stir-fry until wilted, about 1 minute. Stir in sesame seeds and sesame oil. Serve hot, optionally with rice or noodles.

Tip

Preparation note: Have all ingredients ready before starting, as the cooking process is quick.
